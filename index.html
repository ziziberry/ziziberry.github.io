What Is A USGSO?

Unrecognized Single Gender Social Organization. According to the Final Report of the Implementation Committee for the Policy on Membership in Single Gender Social Organizations released on February 17, 2017, “An Unrecognized Single Gender Social Organization is any selective-membership, singlegender organization, whose primary purpose is social, including but not limited to final clubs, fraternities and sororities, that has a membership that is comprised entirely of Harvard students and/or Harvard alumni. This definition does not include Harvard Recognized Independent Student Organizations (ISOs), Sponsored Student Organizations (SSOs)..., or other non-Harvard entities whose membership draws from individuals beyond the Harvard community such as city-wide chapters of national organizations...”
